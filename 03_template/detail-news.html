Focus Universal Inc., a publicly traded company (OTCQB: FCUV), is a universal smart device developer and manufacturer headquartered in Los Angeles, CA

Focus Universal features a Universal Smart Instrumentation Platform (USIP), which provides a unique and universal solution for embedded design, industrial control and monitoring. It achieves this using a mobile device or computer as a display to communicate with a group of sensors/probes. These sensor modules can be produced by any manufacturer and integrated seamlessly with our USIP into our Smart App. This patented technology replaces expensive, limited technology instruments with an inexpensive, singular device that can communicate seamlessly while providing advanced monitoring and control.
