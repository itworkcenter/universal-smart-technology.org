    AGORACOM Online Gold and Commodities Conference

    The AGORACOM Online Gold and Commodities Conference focuses on emerging small and mid-cap resource companies. Companies present by invitation only and are required to have demonstrated excellence through a combination of one or more of experienced management, proven reserves, strong prospective projects, balance sheet strength or actual production. View Presenting Companies

    Presenting companies, keynote speakers and workshop presenters will cover relevant industries that include precious metals, base metals, rare metals, oil & gas, renewable energy, coal and clean energy technologies. With 30 public companies and 7 of the best speakers in the world presenting solely via the web, The AGORACOM Online Gold & Commodities Conference is already being billed as a paradigm shifting event that will change the nature of conferences forever.
